What is QR Code?

A QR Code is an acronym for Quick Response Code. The feature is similar to well-known bar codes but it can contain much more information.

How do I read and access a QR Code?

There are so many QR Codes available to access different information. These are square-shaped black & white, quick-response codes that can be easily accessed with smartphones. If you want to read the QR Code, first you need a QR Code application on your smartphone and then open the application equipped with a camera. When you open the application, you’ll see your phone’s camera become a scanner, and you need to scan the QR Code with your camera, and you’ll see complete details stored in the QR Code. But make sure you need to cover the entire QR Code to scan it properly.

What are QR Code used for?

QR Codes are used for various purposes. With QR Code, you can link to websites, store any Information, provide contact details, or store any “call to action” like dialing the phone, sending instant messages, installing apps, payment processes, etc. There are a lot more possibilities for using QR codes. Previously these QR Codes were used to check product supply chain and food packaging, but now QR Codes are used in business, marketing, advertising, and many more.

Is it safe to use a QR Code?

QR codes are designed with the purpose to help users obtain information or take action easily. However, they can be corrupted just like any other digital tool, so be careful not to engage with suspicious organizations, apps, or websites.
